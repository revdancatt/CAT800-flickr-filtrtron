Others

A whole bunch of photos have machine tag filter names that aren't in the US English Flickr App. Some of these because they were used in the early builds of the App, [alice], [cross process], [vintage] and so on.

Others are translated versions of the filter names, such as camaleón, camaleão, caméléon and chamäleon for the chameleon filter. This makes is harder for us to work out the correct total number of photos with that filter applied without a bunch of faffing around.

Fortunately future versions of the Flickr App should unify the filter names used for the machine tags, the visible translated names will stay as they are. So proportionally over time the significance of the following filters will reduce.
